Benchmarks

Provide a test platform to evaluate AI performance on various hardwares

Test AI models on Edge devices and Mobile SoCs with consistent measurement of accuracy, speed and efficiency


Assess inference speed for key deep learning models and photography applications

Object detection and classification, semantic segmentation, image/video analysis and enhancement, depth and pose estimation, biomedical image


Validate device’s capacity to run traditional computer vision (i.e., CNNs) and new LLMs models

Validate performance in different settings (high/low resolutions; model precision FP8, FP16, INT8, INT16; parallel execution; etc)

Check feasibility in running large models such as ViT-e (~4B params), SAM, Bert (~110M), DeiT (~86M params)


Test hardware capabilities and limitations for different models implementations

Check inference time, use of memory, and power consumption

Test device performance for large and small models

Up-to-date evaluations based on the main state-of-the-art models



Resultados detalhados

Obtidos na execução em 5 telefones e 2 módulos de desenvolvimento de SoCs. O número indicado nas células representa o tempo de inferência do modelo, quantização e meio para execução (CPU, GPU ou NNAPI) representado em cada coluna e no telefone indicado em cada linha.
